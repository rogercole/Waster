How it works

We follow a simple, systems engineering-led process that starts with your paddocks and ends with usable data.

  1. Walk the farm & define what matters
    We visit your property (or review detailed maps) to understand water, soil, stock movement and infrastructure. Together we prioritise what to monitor, which risks to reduce and what decisions you want to make faster.
  2. Design the sensor & network layout
    Using long-range LoRaWAN, we map out gateways, sensors and power so coverage is reliable and practical to maintain – even in remote corners of the farm.
  3. Install, commission & validate
    Hardware is installed on-farm, data is checked, and alerts are tuned to your thresholds. You get training on the dashboards and mobile access.
  4. Monitor, refine & support
    Once the system is live, we help refine alert logic, reporting and integrations (e.g. with existing control systems, farm records, and management platforms) and provide ongoing support.
